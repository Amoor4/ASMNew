Water & Sewerage Works

ASM for engineering and technilogies has great experience in pressurized piping , gravity networks, roads, wtp & wwtp ASM has planned new areas, cities, compounds, and industrials cities where it is essential to plan and locate every componant of the infrastructure and study its effect on the future and operation uses.
Services of water/ wastewater works:- Design of water network pipes, calculate the friction of liquid and velocities and pressures in accordance with designs codes.
Design sewer networks pipes, calculate water quantities, max. and min. velocities and choose the best slope for pipes in accordance with deigns codes. Draw plans and profiles for the projects with full details and here are some of our previous projects.
Design or water treatment plants wastewater plant & pump stations. Previous projects:-
Projects in Egypt: - Building up databases of water distribution network and its accessories of Behiera Governate in Egypt (Area = 9826 km2, population of 6.2 Million, the Model contains more than 50000 pipes and 18 Water pumping stations) with coordination of GIS Department and follow up the updating of these databases with the different branches of Behiera water company.
- Development of water networks databases to be applicable with hydraulic analysis application.
- Design carrier water pipelines in Rahmania, Kafr Aldawar, Abu Hommos, Edfina & Desones.
- Preparing of hydraulic models and studies for the project of Mahmodia water networks (90 Km) pipe lines & water treatment Plant.
- Preparing of hydraulic models and studies for the project of Kafr El Dawar water networks (80 Km) pipe lines.
- Preparing of hydraulic models and studies for the project of Abo Hommos water networks (80 Km) pipe lines & water treatment Plant.
- Preparing of hydraulic models and studies for the project of Nubaria Industrial Area networks.
- Preparing of Sewer networks planes, Hydraulic models, pump stations and & wastrwater treatment Plant. for new sewer network projects for villages (Abu Seif & Messen) in Behiera governorate funded by Social Fund for Development.
- Making of network shop drawing for Irragation 1200 mm prestressed concrete pipe (6.5 Km) in Ismalia governate. - Making of network shop drawing for Irragation 800 mm, Water 600mm & Force main 1300 mm HDPE pipes, and Sewer 600mm, 1000mm, 1200mm Corrugated HDPE pipes (10 Km) in Port Said governate.
Projects in Pakistan: - Preparing of hydraulic models and studies for the project of Muzzerfabad University water, irrigation, stormwater, Sewer, & water treatment Plant, pump stations & wastewater treatment plant networks in Kashmir.
- Preparing of hydraulic models and studies for the project of Bagh District water, irrigation, stormwater & Sewer networks in Kashmir.
- Preparing of hydraulic models and studies for the project of Rawlakot District water, irrigation, stormwater & Sewer networks in Kashmir.
Projects in Saudi Arabia: - Preparing of hydraulic models and studies for the project of Morouj Compound water & Sewer networks in Alehssa.
Projects in United States of America. - Design of Force main pipe line and choose the suitable pump for a project in St. Simons Island in Glynn Country Georgia, United States.
